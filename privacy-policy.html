Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information related to our VPN application is managed, with particular attention to the use of AdMob, Google’s advertising platform. As the developer, technore24 is dedicated to ensuring transparency, user trust, and a seamless, secure experience while respecting and upholding privacy rights.

Data Collection

We prioritize user anonymity and do not collect any personally identifiable data. This means no sensitive information such as device IDs, location details, or any other data that could personally identify you is gathered by our application.

Use of AdMob

Our VPN app incorporates AdMob to serve in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system version, and general usage patterns. This information is anonymized and is used solely to improve ad relevance and performance.

Users have the ability to manage their data preferences, including:

  • Reviewing the non-personal information collected by AdMob.
  • Opting out of data collection and personalized ads via AdMob.
  • Refer to Google’s Partner Sites Policy for further details.

Data Usage and Retention

We do not store or retain any user data on our systems. AdMob processes non-personal data temporarily to ensure effective ad delivery and optimization.

Information Sharing

Your data is not shared with any third parties. Non-personal and aggregated data may be processed by AdMob strictly for the purpose of enhancing advertisement relevance and effectiveness.

Data Security

Protecting your data is a top priority. While we do not collect personal data, we ensure that our systems and third-party integrations follow industry-standard security protocols to protect your information.
